Poop Deck

[north: 217] [down: 208]

You are on the Poop Deck of this mystical boat, and you realise, from looking, that the boat has arrived at its designed destination. A gangplank leads north into a blanket of Mist (a rather patchy blanket), inviting you to step into this higher plane... The steps still lead down to the upper deck, but im afraid that this was a one way journey only, no chance of getting out there!
