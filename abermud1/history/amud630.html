Forests Edge

[north: 625] [east: 626] [south: 662] [west: 629]

You are standing amidst a few trees on the edge of the forest, hedged in by a sheer rock wall to the south, and a steep, but climbable, bank to the east. Elsewhere the forest thickens up and continues. A narrow path climbs along a twisty ledge along the cliff to the south.
