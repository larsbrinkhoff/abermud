Path

[west: 1011]

You are following a path up a cliff. The path ends abruptly to the east where it used to cross a ravine on a bridge. The bridge has been phasered by some vandal, who seems to have been busy on the cliff too.

A copy of Kirk's face has been cut into the cliff along with a large collection of rude messages about hobbits, which are too rude to repeat here.
