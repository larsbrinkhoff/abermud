Dusty Airduct

[east: 201] [west: 126]

You climb into the airduct and find that the atmosphere is hot and clammy. You can hear computer buzz coming from both ends of the airduct and neither reassures you of your safety. The ventilation system itself is old and noisy, and would have many tributary airways leading into and out of this pipe if Moog the creator had enough time to write them...so remind him please!

The airduct leads east and west.
