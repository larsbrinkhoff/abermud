The Bedroom

[east: 540]

You are in a ladies bedroom, sumptuously furnished in pink.. A huge pink double bed lies in the centre of the room, above which are brightly polished mirrors. Make-up and perfume is arranged tastefully on the dresser next to a small envelope on which is written the words "To Fiona- love Dave"

Another note lies here reading: Still unfinished, but there is a bit more now... ----) White the Creator

A door leads east.
