As you step up out of the flame you find yourself in a room of grey stone. There is no sign of a way back down to the temple, and you are not even sure that you would survive such a journey twice anyway as you feel quite shakey and weak after the trip upwards.

You reorientate yourself and notice that the floor of this room has been made into a complex pattern of colour and shapes, quite literally straining your eyes to look at.

Exits from here are south through a door.
