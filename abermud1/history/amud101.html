Entrance to Woods

[north: 102] [east: 109] [south: 108] [west: 100]

You find yourself in a large Wood, and the sound of wild creatures echo around you...nearby a small sign floats in mid air, just out of reach...

Welcome To Doiley Woods. Please do not feed the Moog. (Thankyou)

The Wood fills you with a feeling of happiness, as though hours of contented viewing were stored up here.

A Misty path leads off out of the wood to the west, and in through the Wood to the north, south and east.
