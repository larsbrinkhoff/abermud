[south: 167] [west: 176]

You are in what appears to have been the guard room for the pyramid.

Rows of bunks (for about thirty men) fill one half of the room, whilst the other has a long table for eating at. Some cutlery, or its ancient equivalent, still remains on the table, and the decayed form of food can be seen. This room has a considerable dust covering, but what makes this room make you feel uneasy is the tracks leading through the dust...not even a form of foot that you have seen before!

Exits lead south and west.
