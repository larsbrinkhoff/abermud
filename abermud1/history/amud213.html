Lower Deck

[north: 214] [up: 208]

You are in the lower deck, which were this a mortal creation, would be the crews quarters, but in this boat it is completely empty. There are mystical paintings on the walls, showing scenes relating to the dead king.

A heavy gold door forms the northern wall, and a porthole in the eastern wall shows you the ground far, far below you, and steps lead up to the top deck.
