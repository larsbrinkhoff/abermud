Famous Four Room

[north: 203] [east: 5] [south: 30] [west: 111]

You feel quite humble, and bow down when you enter this room. On each of the the four walls you can see a massive, floodlight, gold framed, portrait of one of the four creators of this game, namely... Anarchy the Cardboard Box on the east wall, White the Creator on the southern wall, Atropos the Wanderer on the western wall, and Moog the Guitar Wizard on the northern wall.

This room is obviously a shrine to the four greatest creators in this universe, and I would strongly advise no attempt at defacing it, because the last person to try this is now stretched out on the floor (one Hobbit) for you to tread on...what else are hobbits for? You can hear whimpers of pain from the creature, but cannot seem to reach it!

You hear the words 'Good Luck! hee hee hee hee!' whispered in your ear as you stand here, but cannot find the source, was it one of the wizards, or even one of the Creators, for they see and know all!

Exits lead off in all four directions through veils of Mist, and you begin to wonder what the Creators have in mind for you...
