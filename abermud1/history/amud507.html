The Pump Room

[west: 509]

This tiny room is dominated by a strange looking brighly coloured machine which is busy pumping caramel, GUNK and molten chocolate around the factory. A big lever stands in front of the machine.

There is a note tied to the lever with Acne Non-Breakable Tape reading:

The lever does nothing right now, but watch this space! White the Creator

The only exit is west onto the factory floor
