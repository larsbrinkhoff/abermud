Blue Moon Detective Agency (reception)

[north: 141] [east: 139] [south: 142] [west: 146]

You have emerged into a totally synthetic universe, created for your conveience in this part of the adventure (well you wouldnt like to be put up against the real thing would you!!).

You find yourself in the reception area of the famous Blue Moon Detective Agency, although it is after office hours and so it is devoid of life. You can see the reception desk where Miss Depesto works, and the various desks at which the other staff work, and limbo-dance at.

A door in the north wall leads into Maddies office, a door in the south wall leads into David's office, whilst the main door takes you west and a glowing blue portal (as yet unseen in the series) shimers to the east.
