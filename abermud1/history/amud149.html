Going down onto all fours...

[south: 148]

Going down onto all fours you manage to crawl into and along this small side tunnel. It is evidently a recent digging, and quite soon you reach the end. In front of you is a rock fall, completely blocking the tunnel, and there is no way forward at all (yet!!...Moog the Creator). The only available exit is back south down the tunnel.

Exits lead south only.
