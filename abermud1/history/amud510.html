The Store Room

[north: 512] [east: 509] [down: 598]

Hundreds of empty barrels stand here in the gloom, sticky with treacle, around a vast well in the centre of the room from the depths of which can occasionally be heard a faint *gloop* sound.

A sign on the wall says:

Hello to all my fans out there, Rachel for the coffee, her room-mate Fiona for not blackmailing me and all the others... hehehehehe

And a real big thankyou to "Tracy"

You can go east only, to the factory floor.
