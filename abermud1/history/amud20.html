High Above The Valley

[north: 90] [east: 489] [south: 5]

You are standing on a narrow path winding down southwards through the trees into a picturesque mountain valley. Trees line the green slopes giving way to a fresh and gleaming coat of snow on the mountain peaks that stare menacingly down from around you. A narrow, litle used path winds east. To the south the path enters a cave, between two beautifully carved statues of nikki..
